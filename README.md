# Getting Started
The basics of magic rituals and your first tools.
### Ritual Dagger
> The Ritual Dagger is your primary tool for creating and managing magic rituals.
> Right-click with the dagger to open the Magic Circle Selection screen, where you can choose a magic circle type to place at your feet. Shift + Right-click to open the Radius Selection screen, where you can place a Magic Center Block with a chosen radius.

# Magic Center
The heart of your ritual setup - fuel, control, and radius management
### What is a Magic Center
> The Magic Center Block is the invisible anchor of your ritual setup. It defines the radius in which magic circles operate and provides the fuel they need to function.
> To place one, Shift + Right-click with the Ritual Dagger and choose a radius . The center is placed beneath your feet.The center block is invisible but shows red particle effects along its radius. It breaks very easily and does not drop anything.

# Magic Circles
Different types of magic circles and their effects.
### Circle Types
>There are three types of magic circles, each granting a different effect to entities standing on them. Multiple circles of the same type within a Magic Center's radius will stack their effects.
- > Attack Red - Grants Strength I. Each additional Attack circle increases the amplifier by one level.
- > Defense Blue - Grants Resistance I. Extra Defense circles increase the resistance level.
- > Regeneration Green - Grants Regeneration I. More Regeneration circles increase the regeneration amplifier.
> The effects are applied passively every tick as long as the Magic Center has fuel.

# Spirit Resources
All the info you need to be rich!
### Spirit Ore
> Spirit Ore is a resource found deep underground in large veins. It must be mined with an Iron Pickaxe or better to drop Spirit Stones.
> Spirit Ore Veins generate between Y=0 and Y=-100 . Each vein is very large, containing 200-250 ore blocks. Mining through a vein may reveal Spirit Vein Centers. Use a Spirit Map or /locate poi magic_ritual_mod:spirit_vein_center command to find nearby veins.
